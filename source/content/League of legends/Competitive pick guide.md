# Mid Lane Champion Pick Guide — Competitive Amateur League

**Pool:** Taliyah, Orianna, Aurora, Galio, Twisted Fate, Pantheon, Soraka mid

---

# Quick Decision Rules

| Champion          | Pick When                                 | Role in Team / Objective         |
| ----------------- | ----------------------------------------- | -------------------------------- |
| [[#Taliyah]]      | Safe/moderate lanes, want roaming + tempo | Roam / objective control         |
| [[#Orianna]]      | Enemy poke / hard lane, teamfight needed  | Wave control + teamfight utility |
| [[#Aurora]]       | Safe lane, want controlled pushes         | Roaming + lane pressure          |
| [[#Galio]]        | Hard poke / burst lane, need frontline    | Tank / engage / anti-poke        |
| [[#Twisted Fate]] | Map-wide pick pressure, lane manageable   | Roaming, pick setup              |
| [[#Pantheon]]     | Aggressive lane, low mobility enemy       | Early lane dominance / roam      |
| [[#Soraka Mid]]   | Enemy poke, sustain needed                | Lane sustain / team heal         |

---

# Taliyah

**When to Pick:**  
- Safe or moderately difficult matchup (not extreme poke/all-in).  
- Need wave control, roaming potential, and tempo.  

**Strengths:**  
- Roaming / creating tempo pressure.  
- Fast wave crashes to enable objectives.  
- Strong zone control with Q+E combo.  

**Team Composition Fit:**  
- Teams needing early-mid lane roam pressure.  
- Pair with: tank jungler (Sejuani, Zac), strong side-lane carries (Fiora, Jax), disengage support (Nautilus, Thresh).  

**Example Composition:**  
- Taliyah mid  
- Zac jungle  
- Fiora top  
- Jinx ADC  
- Nautilus support  

---

# Orianna

**When to Pick:**  
- Enemy mid has long-range poke / zoning mages (Lux, Xerath, Vel’Koz).  
- Need precision wave control and safer laning.  

**Strengths:**  
- Safe vs poke lanes.  
- Strong zone control and teamfight utility with R (Shockwave).  
- Can farm safely under pressure.  

**Team Composition Fit:**  
- Teams needing teamfight utility and disengage.  
- Pair with: AoE frontline (Galio, Maokai), secondary wombo (Wukong, Amumu), strong backline ADC.  

**Example Composition:**  
- Orianna mid  
- Amumu jungle  
- Maokai top  
- Kai’Sa ADC  
- Leona support  

---

# Aurora

**When to Pick:**  
- Lane is manageable; enemy mid not extreme poke/all-in.  
- Want controlled push / roams without heavy risk.  

**Strengths:**  
- Wave manipulation / slow push.  
- Mid-range poke and utility for roaming.  

**Team Composition Fit:**  
- Teams that want mid priority + side-lane pressure.  
- Works with roaming junglers (Elise, Nidalee) and split-push top laners.  

**Example Composition:**  
- Aurora mid  
- Elise jungle  
- Camille top  
- Jhin ADC  
- Lulu support  

---

# Galio

**When to Pick:**  
- Enemy mid is high poke or heavy AP burst.  
- Need lane stability and teamfight initiation.  

**Strengths:**  
- Hard to punish in lane.  
- Strong global presence with R (Hero’s Entrance).  
- Frontline for teamfights, peel for carries.  

**Team Composition Fit:**  
- Poke-heavy enemies.  
- Pair with: strong engage top (Ornn), front-to-back ADC (Miss Fortune), peel supports (Janna, Lulu).  

**Example Composition:**  
- Galio mid  
- Hecarim jungle  
- Ornn top  
- Miss Fortune ADC  
- Janna support  

---

# Twisted Fate

**When to Pick:**  
- Want map-wide pressure / pick potential.  
- Lane is manageable or wave already controlled.  
- Best for team-oriented rotations.  

**Strengths:**  
- Roam and pick potential anywhere on map.  
- Card selection for utility (gold card CC).  

**Team Composition Fit:**  
- Teams that need early map dominance / coordinated plays.  
- Works with hard-engage jungler (Jarvan IV, Sejuani) and side-lane threats.  

**Example Composition:**  
- Twisted Fate mid  
- Jarvan IV jungle  
- Renekton top  
- Jinx ADC  
- Nautilus support  

---

# Pantheon

**When to Pick:**  
- Want lane dominance and roam threat.  
- Enemy mid is low mobility / skill-shot reliant.  

**Strengths:**  
- Early all-in / lane kill pressure.  
- Global ultimate for cross-map plays.  

**Team Composition Fit:**  
- Aggressive, snowball-oriented comps.  
- Pair with scaling ADC (Jinx, Kai’Sa), tank top (Shen), utility jungler.  

**Example Composition:**  
- Pantheon mid  
- Lee Sin jungle  
- Shen top  
- Jinx ADC  
- Thresh support  

---

# Soraka Mid

**When to Pick:**  
- Enemy poke-heavy AP scaling.  
- Want lane sustain and team healing.  
- Very niche, mostly for amateur comps.  

**Strengths:**  
- Sustain for team and lane.  
- Denies enemy poke.  
- Can pair with high-damage melee jungler for aggressive trades.  

**Team Composition Fit:**  
- Sustained, poke-heavy comps that rely on healing.  
- Pair with divers / melee assassins (Darius, Lee Sin) and scaling ADC.  

**Example Composition:**  
- Soraka mid  
- Lee Sin jungle  
- Darius top  
- Jinx ADC  
- Leona support
